Fate Grand Order

What is Fate Grand Order and why should I play it?

Fate is a massive franchise with tons of different anime, games, and movies. One of their most popular releases was this mobile game, "Fate Grand Order," and it got so popular that it surpassed Fortnite in tweets on Twitter in 2019. It's a great time killer and the storyline and events are always interesting. There's always something new to do in the game, whether it be to grind to level up your characters or to farm on the new event. If you can get into the game, it becomes immensely fun, and the community is generally kind and supportive. The gameplay is simple, there's a well thought-out storyline, and the art is high quality. What more could you ask for?
