Lancers


  • Lancers have a base damage multiplier of 1.05x.

  • Lancers have a base star generation rate of 12%.

  • Lancers have a base star absorption of 90.

  • Lancers have a base death rate of 40%.

  • Archers take double damage from Lancers and deal half damage against them.

  • Sabers take half damage from Lancers and deal double damage against them.

  • Berserkers take double damage from Lancers and deal 50% extra damage against them.

  • Rulers take half damage from Lancers.

  • Alter Egos deal half damage to Lancers.

  • Beast I deals double damage to Lancers.
