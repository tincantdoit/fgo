Riders


  • Riders have a base damage multiplier of 1.0x.

  • Riders have a base star generation rate of 9%.

  • Riders have a base star absorption of 200.

  • Riders have a base death rate of 50%.

  • Casters take double damage from Riders and deal half damage against them.

  • Assassins take half damage from Riders and deal double damage against them.

  • Berserkers take double damage from Riders and deal 50% extra damage against them.

  • Rulers take half damage from Riders.

  • Alter Egos deal 50% extra damage to Riders.

  • Beast I takes double damage from Riders.
