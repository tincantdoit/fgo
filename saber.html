Sabers


  • Sabers have a base damage multiplier of 1.0x.

  • Sabers have a base star generation rate of 10%.

  • Sabers have a base star absorption of 100.

  • Sabers have a base death rate of 35%.

  • Lancers take double damage from Sabers and deal half damage against them.

  • Archers take half damage from Sabers and deal double damage against them.

  • Berserkers take double damage from Sabers and deal 50% extra damage against them.

  • Rulers take half damage from Sabers.

  • Alter Egos deal half damage to Sabers.

  • Beast I deals double damage to Sabers.
